Who Are We?

Finding gifts for friends, family members, coworkers, or significant others is hard. In fact, most people hate it and spend too much time on it. At Gifty, we want to streamline the gift giving process, utilizing social media to help you find the perfect gift!

How Does it Work?


Step 1: Creating a profile through Facebook allows us to gather crucial information about you such as the books, movies, sports teams, celebrities, and places you enjoy.

Step 2: Refer friends to Gifty. You can find gifts for any Facebook friend with a Gifty profile. Just indicate a price range, and general type of gift, and Gifty does the brainstorming for you.

Step 3: Search through dozens of gift ideas ranging from experiences to products and find the perfect gift! We'll never display adds or endorse brands.
